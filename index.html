Vision

To create a platform where all our Nepalese people can connect to each other where they can share their own business idea along with their daily struggles like if they need any medical assistance or diplomatic support.


This platform would be a entrepreneneur's platform where people can share their business ideas and request for capital in order to establish it anywhere around the globe. We would provide the funds and all the required documentation process to help them for setting up their business in the preferred locations

About Us

1+ Solution is the platform where nepalese people working in U.A.E connect to each other for help, support and grow together. It creates many opportunities to all the nepalese people in U.A.E. The name 1+ means to make connection and union between nepalese people. Along with that it also symbolise for every problem there is a solution. As many people are facing different problem in foreign land this platform helps to solve the problem with the proper solution. Therefore connecting nepalese people for exchanging thoughts, idea and creating strong bond to develop prosperous and build up together. So it is named 1+ Solution, which also says that while problem rise solution for that will also rise.
